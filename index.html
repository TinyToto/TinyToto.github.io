  • National Undergraduate Innovation Research Program

    2014/06-2015/06

    Supervisor: Prof.Xie, Hui. PhD (Shef eld), MIOA

    Investigate the noise exposure of residential areas along LRT lines in Chongqing, China, through a series of eld measurements, questionnaires surveys and statistic analysis.

    Authored analytical demographic report, which classi ed eight typical spatial con gurations to represent the relationship between LRT lines and urban mountainous environment in Chongqing.

    Analyzed data from filed measurements with Principal Components Analysis and statistic software SPSS, our research showed that citizen’s noise sensitivity and age might signi cantly affect LRT noise annoyance and impact, whereas window glazing and residence time have no signi cant in uence.
